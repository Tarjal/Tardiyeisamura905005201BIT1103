About Us

Our Story

Football Agency Sierra Leone was established in 2015 with a vision to transform the football landscape in Sierra Leone. We recognized the immense talent in our country but noticed a lack of professional representation and pathways for these players to reach their full potential.

Founded by former national team player Alhaji Kamara, our agency started with just three players and a dream. Today, we represent over 30 professional footballers across Sierra Leone's premier league and international competitions.

Over the years, we have grown to become the premier football agency in Sierra Leone, representing the country's most promising football talents.

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and promote Sierra Leonean football talent by providing professional representation, career guidance, and opportunities that enable players to achieve their full potential both on and off the field.

  • Provide comprehensive player management services
  • Facilitate international exposure and opportunities
  • Develop youth talent through structured programs
  • Promote professional conduct and education

Our Vision

To become the leading football agency in West Africa, recognized for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and the development of world-class football talent from Sierra Leone.

  • Establish partnerships with European clubs
  • Create a sustainable talent development pipeline
  • Increase international transfers for our players
  • Contribute to Sierra Leone's football development
